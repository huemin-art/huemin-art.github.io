Model and Methods

Through a process of trial and error, the generator becomes better at producing images that fool the discriminator into thinking they are real. This process can be repeated numerous times until the generated images are of high quality and indistinguishable from real images. GANs have been used to create a wide range of AI-generated art, including paintings, photographs, and even 3D models.

About huemin

huemin is a digital AI artist and programmer who discovered a passion for AI art as a medium in August 2021. Since then, huemin has been creating digital artworks that showcase a unique blend of color, minimalism, and geometric abstraction.

As an innovator in the AI art space, huemin has not only created a notable body of work but has made significant contributions to the open-source community. huemin has developed AI art tools that have been widely used by other artists, including deforum, a free to use stable diffusion animation inference tool.
